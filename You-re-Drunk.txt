**You're Drunk**
by jhoohoo

Ayowe awxewr nwaalfw die tiy rgw fklf ua xgixiklrw! Tiy lew qwkxinw.

The cipher here is a substitution cipher where every letter is substitued with the key to the right of the letter on a normal keyboard.

For example, Ayowe = Super

You can also utilize websites like https://quipqiup.com/ to solve simple substitution ciphers like this. 

The original message is: Super secret message for you the glag is chocolate! You are welcome.

Hence the "f"lag is "chocolate".
